## Revenue Model

### Freemium Pricing
- **Free**: Basic features, 10 AI messages/day, ads
- **Premium** ($4.99/month): Unlimited AI, advanced analytics, no ads
- **Pro** ($9.99/month): Everything + 1-on-1 tutoring, video sessions

### Break-Even Analysis (1,000 users)

**Monthly Cost**: $78.50

**Revenue (10% Premium + 2% Pro)**:
- 100 Premium × $4.99 = $499
- 20 Pro × $9.99 = $199.80
- Total: $698.80/month
- Profit: $620.30/month

**Break-even**: Only 16 Premium subscribers needed

## Cost Optimization

1. Use free tiers maximally
2. Implement AI response caching
3. Limit free tier AI messages
4. Optimize database queries
5. Batch email notifications
6. Monitor usage closely

## Summary

- **Start**: $1.25/month (free tiers)
- **Scale**: Costs grow with users
- **Revenue**: High profit margin (80%+)
