## Design Principles

### 1. User-Focused
- Speak to student needs, not features
- Benefits over specifications
- Clear value proposition

### 2. Community-First
- Highlight social features
- Emphasize collaboration
- Show organization support

### 3. Clean & Simple
- Remove technical jargon
- Focus on what matters
- Easy to scan

### 4. Trust Building
- Free forever messaging
- No credit card required
- Social proof elements
